Early Warning Signal System
3-tier framework · SHAP-driven triggers
🟡 WATCH Monthly
Trigger: DTI > 0.50 OR spending_shock = 1
Action: Flag for monitoring; send repayment reminder
🟠 ALERT Bi-weekly
Trigger: DTI > 0.55 AND (installment/income > 0.35 OR cash_flow_volatility > 0.75)
Action: Proactive outreach; offer restructuring options
🔴 CRITICAL Weekly
Trigger: Grade ≥ E AND DTI > 0.55 AND spending_shock = 1
Action: Immediate escalation to collections team
